## Ellipsoidal Regions
#### Circles
`centerX`;`centerY`;`radius`
#### Ellipses
`centerX`;`centerY`;`radius1`;`radius2`
#### Examples
```csv
-3.5;4;3
5.0;-2.77;6;2
```

## Polygonal Regions
#### Rectangles
`point1X`;`point1Y`;`point2X`;`point2Y`

#### Triangles
`point1X`;`point1Y`;`point2X`;`point2Y`;`point3X`;`point3Y`

#### Other polygons
`point1X`;`point1Y`;`point2X`;`point2Y`;...;`pointnX`;`pointnY`
